
A user's first impression of a personal website is highly visual. Even a simple text-focused portfolio can feel premium and state of the art if styled with the right details.

### Palette Selection
Avoid plain saturated colors (like raw red or blue). Instead, leverage **OKLCH** color spaces to define responsive, uniform-contrast palettes:
- High-contrast, clean dark modes.
- Light modes with soft, off-white background fills (`oklch(99% .003 240)`).

### Subtle Micro-animations & Hovers
To make the site feel alive, introduce subtle physical properties:
*   **Elevation transitions**: Smooth shadow and translate adjustments when hovering over cards.
*   **Rainbow borders**: Gradient border transitions that animate or light up under cursor hover, giving that sleek Gemini-like aesthetic.
*   **Dynamic text accents**: Gradient-based text highlights for links.

By loading local, high-quality typography (like **Outfit**) and avoiding heavy third-party assets, we can keep Lighthouse performance at 100 while looking stunning.
